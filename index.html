Welcome to Leemore & Associates, connect with us.

LEEMORE & ASSOCIATES Real Estate Appraisers & Consultants Ltd. is a medium size residential Real Estate appraisal company that has been in practice since 1994 and officially established in 1997. The company is situated in Coquitlam and offers a full range of residential appraisal services throughout the Lower Mainland and the Fraser Valley.

All of our appraisers are members of the Appraisal Institute of Canada (AIC) as well as several members of the Real Estate Institute of BC (RI). Our Appraisers hold professional designations of AACI P.App (Accredited Appraiser of the Canadian Institute,) or CRA (Canadian Residential Appraiser) or are working towards these designations.

CLient Services

LEEMORE & ASSOCIATES Real Estate Appraisers & Consultants Ltd. offers professional service throughout the Lower Mainland to Whistler / Pemberton and the Fraser Valley to Langley. We provide our clients with a high quality appraisal report due to years of experience and the latest in appraisal computer software and on-line data services. Thorough research while completing the report is essential to ensuring an accurate and credible appraised value.

Our appraisers have the best contacts in the Real Estate industry and government agencies.

They are also well known with builders and developers within their primary areas.

Our “turn-around time” for residential appraisals is typically within 24 hours of viewing the property, often with "verbal" confirmations even sooner. Our appraisers can be reached through the office number at any time and during off business hours the messages are checked regularly. Contact Us.
